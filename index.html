FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about GrowingYourWork before you get started.

How does GrowingYourWork work?
GrowingYourWork is a two-sided marketplace. Freelancers create a profile and browse or receive matched job opportunities. Companies post jobs or search for talent. Both can communicate, agree on terms, and work together — all within the platform.
Is it free to sign up as a freelancer?
Yes! Creating a profile and browsing jobs is completely free. Our Starter plan gives you up to 5 job applications per month at no cost. Upgrade to Professional for unlimited access and advanced features.
How does GrowingYourWork verify freelancer quality?
We use a multi-layer verification system: identity checks, skills assessments, portfolio reviews, and ongoing client rating monitoring. Only freelancers who maintain high performance standards remain featured on the platform.
What payment methods are supported?
We support all major payment methods including credit/debit cards, bank transfers, PayPal, Stripe, and cryptocurrency. Payments are held in escrow and released once work is approved, ensuring both parties are protected.
Can I hire for short-term tasks as well as long-term projects?
Absolutely. GrowingYourWork supports everything from a 1-hour quick task to multi-year retainer engagements. Our flexible contract system adapts to fixed-price, milestone-based, and hourly billing arrangements.
What if I'm not satisfied with the work delivered?
Our dispute resolution team is available 24/7. We offer a structured mediation process and, when eligible, full refund protection through our payment guarantee program. Your satisfaction is our priority.
